Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Spaghetti pasta - 200g
  • Eggs - 2
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 50g
  • Pancetta or bacon - 100g
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook pancetta/bacon until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add it to the pan with pancetta/garlic mixture.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture, allowing the heat of the pasta to cook the eggs.
  6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.

Green Smoothie

  • Baby spinach - 2 cups
  • Banana - 1, frozen
  • Almond milk - 1 cup
  • Chia seeds - 1 tablespoon
  • Ice cubes - 1 cup

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
